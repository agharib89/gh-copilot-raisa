Raisa Energy

Raisa Energy is an independent exploration and production company headquartered in Denver, Colorado, with an additional key technology hub in Cairo, Egypt. Founded in 2014 by Luis Rodriguez, the company focuses on acquiring, owning, and leasing mineral and non-operated working interests in oil and gas basins across North America, especially in Colorado, North Dakota, Oklahoma, and Texas.

Technology-Driven Approach

Raisa Energy differentiates itself through innovation and technology. The company has developed proprietary AI-driven forecasting and instant pricing tools that enable rapid, accurate asset evaluation and risk estimation. This tech-forward approach extends to asset management, allowing Raisa to efficiently oversee a large number of assets for multiple investors concurrently.
